Design Reference — Internal

Services Wheel
Concepts

Five alternative renderings of the holistic planning wheel. Each explores a distinct visual language while preserving the same brand identity, service structure, and YOU-centered philosophy.

01
Variant One

Orbit

A single thin circle carries the five services. Dots sit directly on the ring; short spokes draw the eye inward to YOU at center. The most restrained of the five — every mark earns its place.

02
Variant Two

Bare Spokes

No ring — just five lines radiating from YOU. Each spoke ends in a small accent square. Sans-serif type throughout for a more contemporary, open feel. The absence of the circle makes the center feel more deliberate.

03
Variant Three

Pentagon Frame

The five services are connected by a pentagon outline, then anchored inward to YOU by spokes. The frame makes every service feel equal and interrelated. Precise and architectural — no element floats freely.

04
Variant Four

Three Rings

Three concentric rings replace the spoke model entirely. YOU lives inside the innermost. The middle ring is decorative. Services sit on the outer ring. No spokes — the rings do all the organizing. Feels more like a solar system than a diagram.

05
Variant Five

Callout Diagram

Each service "calls out" from the circle with an elbow line, like a technical illustration or anatomy diagram. Feels precise and thoughtful. Communicates that every service is a distinct, identified component of a whole system.
