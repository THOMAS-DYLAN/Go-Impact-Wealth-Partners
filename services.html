Service 02

Protection Planning
& Medicare Insurance

Financial plans can unravel quickly when the unexpected happens — illness, disability, or a long-term care event can erode decades of savings in a short time. Protection planning identifies the risks specific to your situation and builds a coverage strategy designed to protect what you've built.

Impact Wealth Partners works with leading insurance carriers to find the right coverage for your situation. In certain cases where insurance products are implemented, compensation may be received from the carrier — this is always clearly disclosed.

  • Medicare planning and plan selection guidance
  • Life insurance needs analysis and coverage review
  • Disability income insurance for working professionals
  • Long-term care planning for Phoenix-area families
  • Critical illness coverage evaluation

Medicare planning in Phoenix, AZ

Navigating Medicare — Original Medicare, Medicare Advantage, Medicare Supplement, and Part D drug coverage — can be genuinely complex. The right plan depends on your health, your doctors, and your financial situation.

Michael helps clients in the Phoenix, Scottsdale, and greater Arizona area evaluate Medicare options clearly, so you choose coverage that fits your life — not just the cheapest plan on paper.

Service 04

Tax Planning
Coordination & Strategy

Nearly every financial decision has a tax component — investment gains, retirement account withdrawals, Social Security income, estate transfers, and insurance benefits all carry potential tax consequences. Ignoring tax strategy means leaving money on the table.

Michael works in harmony with your accountant or tax professional to integrate tax-smart thinking throughout your financial plan — not just at year-end, but as an ongoing component of every major financial decision.

  • Tax-efficient investment account structure and placement
  • Roth conversion analysis and strategy
  • Required Minimum Distribution (RMD) planning
  • Tax-smart withdrawal sequencing in retirement
  • Coordination with your CPA or tax advisor

Tax strategy in Arizona

Arizona has a flat state income tax structure, making it relatively tax-friendly compared to many states. However, federal tax planning remains just as critical — particularly around retirement account distributions, Social Security income thresholds, Medicare IRMAA surcharges, and capital gains.

Michael's tax planning approach is proactive — building tax efficiency into your financial strategy from the start, rather than reacting at tax time.

Estate Planning

Impact Wealth Partners approaches estate planning as a collaborative process. Michael coordinates with your estate attorney and legal advisors to make sure your financial plan and estate plan work together as a unified whole.

  • Estate plan review and gap analysis
  • Beneficiary designation audit across all accounts
  • Coordination with estate attorneys and legal advisors
  • Wealth transfer strategy for Phoenix-area families
  • Integration of charitable giving goals into your financial plan

Building a lasting legacy in Arizona

Whether you're thinking about passing wealth to children or grandchildren, supporting a cause you believe in, or simply ensuring your spouse is protected — estate planning gives you the control to define your legacy on your own terms.

Michael helps clients across the greater Phoenix area — from Scottsdale and Paradise Valley to Chandler, Tempe, and Mesa — approach estate planning as a meaningful, intentional act rather than a paperwork exercise.
