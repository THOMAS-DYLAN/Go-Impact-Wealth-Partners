Michael Peterson is a CERTIFIED FINANCIAL PLANNER® professional and co-founder of Impact Wealth Partners, serving clients alongside his wife and co-founder, Jill Peterson.

As a fee-based fiduciary advisor, Mike believes financial advice should be transparent, personal, and always aligned with the client’s best interests. His compensation is straightforward, his advice is objective, and his success is measured by one thing: helping clients achieve the life they envision for themselves and their families.

When clients work with Impact Wealth Partners, they work directly with Mike — not a junior associate, not a call center, and not a revolving team of advisors. Every relationship is built on trust, accessibility, and a genuine commitment to understanding what matters most to each client.

His guidance spans retirement income planning, investment management, Medicare and Social Security strategies, tax-efficient distribution planning, estate planning coordination, and long-term wealth preservation — approached as one connected plan, not separate pieces.

The Story Behind the Practice

An advisor who found
his calling.

Michael’s path into financial planning wasn’t linear — but it was deeply intentional. After earning his degree in economics, he opened a small business while working as a bank teller to help support his young family. That opportunity led to a role as a personal banker and eventually to financial advising. Through those experiences, he discovered that his greatest professional fulfillment came from helping people make informed decisions during some of life’s most important transitions.

Known as “Mr. Personable,” Mike has a rare gift for connecting with anyone. He believes every person has a story worth understanding, and that listening well is just as important as giving good advice. Clients describe him as kind, trustworthy, and refreshingly down-to-earth — someone who truly cares, works hard, and always follows through.

Outside the office, Mike is most at home in the great outdoors. He enjoys hiking, big game hunting, and vacations with his family. A lifelong Minnesota Vikings fan — and as a teenager, a ball boy for the team through his uncle, who served as the team’s business manager — he brings that same enthusiasm and loyalty to everything he does.
